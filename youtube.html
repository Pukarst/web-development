HTML YouTube

HTML YouTube embed refers to the practice of inserting a YouTube video directly into a webpage using HTML "iframe" code.It uses the "iframe" HTML element to embed a YouTube video so visitors can watch it without leaving your website.

Use of it

  1. To display videos from YouTube on your website.
  2. No need to host large video files yourself.
  3. Great for tutorials, music, product demos, and more.
