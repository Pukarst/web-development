About Mandala Art


The above image is Mandala Art. Mandala Art is a type of intrincate, circular design that is often used for the spiritual,meditative and artistic purposes. Mandalas are more then just preety patterns-- they often represent the universe, the inner itself or the path to enlightment.

Key Features of Mandala Art:
  1. Most mandalas are created around a central point and grow outward in the symmetrical patterns.
  2. Mandalas usually have repeating shapes,lines and symbols arranged in a balanced way.
  3. In many cultures, mandalas represent the universe,wholeness or the journey of life.
  4. Mandala Art helps to balance and harmony between body,mind and spirit.
